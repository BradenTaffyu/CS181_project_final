2022 stellaris might be the best definitive sci fi experience in gaming it has quite a tough learning curve and will probably take a couple of playthroughs to understand how to play it well but along the way you ll explore loads of well written narratives from researching anomalies you encounter across hundreds of star systems interactions with other alien races and even events that occur by random chance or as a result of your own choices within your own empire this game will humble you through defeat after defeat but the payoff of finally figuring out how to build a strong functioning society in a galaxy of other alien civilizations is unlike any other strategy game i ve ever played
