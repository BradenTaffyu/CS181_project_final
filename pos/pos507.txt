2019 stardew valley is proof a game doesn t require insane graphics to be incredibly enjoyable and addictive it just needs good mechanics it was a breath of fresh air after years playing harvest moon farm sims which though fun all have the same issues somewhat boring archtype characters and only heterosexual marriage options the villagers in stardew seem like real people you would find in a small rural town there are drunks there are homeless people there are people fighting depression it tackles the issue of large corporate entities driving out small town businesses and gives you the option to either help or hinder this effort it does all this while still maintaining a wholesome bright and non stressful atmosphere there are challenging elements like fighting monsters in the mines and mastering the fishing mini game they give you solid goals to work towards so that despite the repetitive elements of the game you always have something to plan out overall just a really really solid farming life sim 10 10
