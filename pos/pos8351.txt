2021 frostpunk is a bitterly cold game every wife crying over the corpse of their frozen husband every father dying in the cold looking for their runaway child every amputation and every sacrifice made for the greater good makes it one of the most miserable experiences you can find at the moment and yet 11bit manages to make such unrelenting suffering an utterly stellar colony simulator and narrative experience that you d be a fool to skip verdict 9 10
