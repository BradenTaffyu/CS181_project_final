2021 early access review caves of qud feels ancient like a cult classic that was only known by a few people in the early 90s it smells of dust and undisturbed basements each iteration you get a little further before dying it becomes apparent at just how deep this game goes a chasm of content that endlessly reforms itself into a new story to die in it is weird science fantasy that s at home in 80s ttrpgs you ll find a box of crayons next to cybernetic implants you ll talk to plants you will honour an village by sitting in their sacred roller chair you ll make deals with ancient war machines your mind will be hunted across time and space your own clone will kill you monkeys will pelt you to death with stones you will die a lot play caves of qud a game that will never be finished
